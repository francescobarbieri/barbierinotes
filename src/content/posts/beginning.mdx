## Why your website is so minimal?

The Internet has become a bloated mess. Huge JavaScript libraries and frameworks that increase client-side load, and many elements like animations, fonts and images, are often unnecessary. 

&nbsp;<br/>
As someone who has always appreciated the unfortunatley now rare feeling of a fast and optimized website, I began questioning myself if this trend was the right direction to take for my own site. Unsurprisingly, it wasn't. 

&nbsp;<br/>
Instead, I dediced to adopt a content-first approach for my website, where trendy elements that lose appal quickly won't be rendered on your screen here.

&nbsp;<br/>
As a collateral benefit, this blog proudly joined the [512kb club](https://512kb.club/), a collection of high-performance and optimized websites. 

## What is the tech stack?

After a bit of research - and developing the website several times - I found that [Astro](https://astro.build/) was the perfect framework for this project.
